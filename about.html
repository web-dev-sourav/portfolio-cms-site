My Story

From Agency Life to
Independent Specialist

I started my career at a top digital marketing agency where I managed millions in META ad spend for Fortune 500 companies. After 4 years, I saw that the brands getting the best results weren't the biggest — they were the ones with focused, specialized expertise behind their ads.

In 2021, I went independent to work exclusively with ecommerce brands — giving them the same quality of attention and expertise that previously only large corporations could afford. Today, I manage META advertising for 20+ active ecommerce clients ranging from $5K to $500K in monthly ad spend.

My edge? I don't just run ads — I understand ecommerce. I know how to optimize for lifetime value, not just front-end ROAS. I understand inventory constraints, contribution margin, and the full picture of what makes a DTC brand profitable.
