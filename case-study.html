📋 The Situation

The Challenge

LuxeSkin Co. is a DTC skincare brand selling premium serums and moisturizers online. When they came to me in January 2025, they had been running META ads for 8 months with dismal results — spending $30,000/month and generating just $39,000 in monthly revenue, a 1.3x ROAS that barely covered ad costs, let alone product, shipping, and overhead.

The founder, Emma, had worked with two other agencies before me. Both had promised ROAS improvement but delivered the same stagnant results. She was on the verge of shutting down the META ads channel entirely and going back to organic social.

🔍 What I Found

The Root Problems

Before touching a single campaign, I spent 5 days doing a full audit. What I found were 4 critical issues killing their performance:

🏗️
Broken Campaign Structure
23 active campaigns with overlapping audiences causing internal auction competition and bloated CPMs.
🎨
Stale, Generic Creative
Every ad used the same lifestyle imagery with product shots. No UGC, no authentic hooks, and no creative rotation in 6 months.
👥
Wrong Audience Targeting
Targeting interest-based audiences that were far too broad and not aligned with the product's true buyer persona.
📊
No Attribution Model
Optimizing on last-click conversions, missing 40% of actual sales driven by META. ROAS was understated in reality.
🗺️ The Plan

The Strategy

I implemented a complete overhaul in three phases over 90 days:

1
Account Architecture Rebuild (Days 1–14)
Consolidated 23 campaigns into 4 clean campaigns following the CBO (Campaign Budget Optimization) structure. Created clear TOFU, MOFU, and BOFU campaign tiers with non-overlapping audience exclusions. This alone reduced CPMs by 35% within 2 weeks.
2
UGC Creative Testing Sprint (Days 7–45)
Worked with 6 UGC creators to produce 18 authentic testimonial-style video ads. Ran a structured creative testing matrix testing: hook variations (problem-aware vs. aspirational), video length (15s, 30s, 60s), and CTA placement. Found the winning formula: 30-second problem/solution UGC video with overlay text hooks.
3
Audience Strategy Overhaul (Days 14–30)
Scrapped interest targeting in favor of: Advantage+ audiences for prospecting, 1% LTV-based Lookalike audiences from top 30-day purchasers, and layered retargeting segments (website visitors → product viewers → add-to-cart → abandoners). Each segment got unique creative tailored to their stage.
4
Scale + Attribution Fix (Days 30–90)
Installed Triple Whale for multi-touch attribution, revealing META was actually responsible for 40% more revenue than last-click showed. With true ROAS data, we confidently scaled daily budget from $1K to $4K on winning campaigns. Revenue compounded monthly as data improved targeting models.

Key Learnings

  • UGC outperformed polished brand creative by 3.2x on CTR and 2.8x on ROAS across every test
  • Audience consolidation reduced CPA by 35% before any creative changes were made
  • Attribution fixing revealed 40% more META-attributed revenue — the ROAS was never as bad as it looked
  • Advantage+ Shopping audiences matched or outperformed manually built Lookalikes at scale
  • Retargeting sequences with tailored creative per stage (visit/cart/checkout) reduced CPA by 61%
  • Daily budget scaling of maximum 20% per 48 hours avoided algorithm resets and maintained efficiency
